Services

There are a variety of Pet Care Services available.

Dog Walking

Friendly, playful human interaction for your pup. A healthy and consistent exercise routine reduces your dog's stress and provides mental stimulation. Daily updates and photos will be provided for you. I'm happy to deliver Medications and Paw-rent approved treats by request. All of this equals a very tired and happy dog! The price includes two pets, the third and up is $5 per pet.

In-Home Visits

Playtime for your furry family member in your home. Cuddles and belly rubs, bathroom breaks, feeding, and fresh water are included. I'm also on poop patrol and cleanup. I can administer medications if necessary, and I will post updates and photos daily.

30 min: $25.00
60 min: $46.00

Pet Sitting

You can have peace of mind knowing your pet is happy and safe while you are away. Your pet will NOT be in a kennel, and you don't have to impose on friends, family, or neighbors. I provide updates and photos for you daily.
